About Me

As a Teaching Assistant at Generation USA, I’m either preparing my next presentation, helping to refine the curriculum, or teaching students how to code and figure out their way through this big space we call tech!


As a Full Stack Web Developer, I design and implement web sites that express a client’s vision as well as deliver an intuitive experience for their visitors.


Previously, I worked in both retail and hospitality in roles that required documentation and improvement of broken systems, teambuilding and servant leadership, and ensuring quality experiences to a wide variety of guests.


In every one of these roles, there's always one, important question: How can I help?
